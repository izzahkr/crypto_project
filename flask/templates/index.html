What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security. It is decentralized and based on blockchain technology, ensuring transparency and security in transactions.

Features of Our Dashboard

  • Real-time data on various cryptocurrencies
  • Visualizations of price trends and market metrics
  • Comparison of multiple cryptocurrencies
  • Interactive charts and tables for better analysis

How to Use the Dashboard

Select a cryptocurrency from the dropdown menu to view its current metrics, including price, market cap, and volume. You can also explore the top 5 cryptocurrencies directly on the dashboard.
